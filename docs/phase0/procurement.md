## Purchase workflow
1. Enter all Tier 1 items into Stanford SPO cart, group by vendor to trigger volume discount.
2. Flag NHS reagents as "hazmat / cold-chain" so purchasing routes through BSL ops.
3. Track receipts in `data_capture_templates.md` → reagent lot log; critical for reproducibility.
4. Reagents with lot-sensitive performance (FBS, MethoCult, cytokines): qualify one lot for entire study — reserve remaining inventory with vendor.
